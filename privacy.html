1. Data we collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to provide RouteFlow London. This includes:

  • Account details: Your email address, display name, and encrypted authentication tokens when you create an account.
  • Preferences: Saved stops, favourite routes, display settings, and notification choices so we can tailor the interface.
  • Usage signals: Aggregated analytics about feature usage that help us improve performance. These signals do not include the precise searches you run or locations you view.
  • Support messages: Any information you choose to provide when you contact us for assistance.

We do not collect live location data, payment information, or content from your private notes unless you explicitly share it with the support team.

2. How we use your data

  • Authenticate you securely and keep your saved stops, notes, and favourites in sync across devices.
  • Deliver personalised views—such as defaulting to your preferred theme or favourite routes—so the interface stays calm and familiar.
  • Monitor the health of our APIs to ensure live data remains accurate and fast.
  • Communicate important updates, such as changes to the network or planned maintenance, when you opt in.

3. When we share data

We only share your information in limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted partners that supply authentication, hosting, and analytics infrastructure. They process data strictly on our instructions.
  • Legal requirements: If we receive a lawful request, we may disclose data to comply with regulations or protect RouteFlow London, our users, or the public.
  • With your permission: We may share information externally if you explicitly ask us to, such as when collaborating on community projects.

We never sell your personal information or monetise it through advertising brokers.

4. Your rights & controls

You can exercise the following rights at any time by contacting us:

  • Request a copy of the data linked to your account.
  • Ask us to update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your account and associated stored data.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications and analytics.

We respond to verified requests within 30 days and keep you updated on progress if additional time is required.

5. How long we keep data

  • Account details and favourites are retained while your account remains active.
  • Support conversations are stored for up to 12 months to help us track recurring issues.
  • Aggregated analytics are kept indefinitely but cannot be traced back to an individual.

When you delete your account we remove associated personal data within 30 days, unless we are legally required to keep it longer.

6. Talk to us

If you have questions about this policy or how we handle your information, please reach out:

  • Email: privacy@routeflow.london
  • Contact form: routeflow.london/contact
  • Discord: RouteFlow London HQ

We review the privacy policy regularly and will let you know about significant updates through the app and newsletter.
