Privacy notice

We respect your data and your trust.

This notice explains what information we collect, how we use it and the choices you have. RouteFlow London follows UK GDPR and keeps sensitive data within Stack Auth.

Information we collect

When you create a RouteFlow account, Stack Auth stores your email, display name and authentication tokens. We gather optional analytics to understand feature usage, but we never sell your data or share it without permission.

How we use your data

  • Provide access to admin dashboards, planning tools and heritage archives.
  • Send notifications about disruptions, mission invites and account updates.
  • Improve reliability through aggregated, anonymised analytics.

Your rights

You can request a copy of your data, ask for corrections or delete your account any time. Contact privacy@routeflow.london and we'll respond within 30 days.
